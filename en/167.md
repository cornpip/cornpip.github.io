## Moving the blog

I'm migrating my [Tistory blog](https://cornpip.tistory.com) over to GitHub Pages.
From now on, new posts will land here at [cornpip.github.io](https://cornpip.github.io).

### Why the move

- **Write in Markdown** — version posts with Git instead of fighting an editor.
- **Code-friendly** — code blocks, highlighting, and math come built in for dev writing.
- **No ads** — a clean, distraction-free reading experience.
- **Ownership** — I manage the content myself, free of platform policy changes.

### What changes

The existing Tistory posts stay put while I migrate them here one by one.
Each migrated post keeps a link back to its original at the top.
